Why Navve

Every great place deserves to be remembered

Navve helps you save, organize, and share the places that matter to you. Add places your way: share directly from Apple Maps, paste an Apple Maps link, drop in any address, or enter details manually.

Save from anywhere

Share directly from Apple Maps — Navve captures the detailed address, assigns a category, and saves the website automatically. Or paste any address, or add a place manually. Zero typing required.

Find it instantly

Search across names, addresses, cities, and categories in real time. Filter by type or country, sort by distance, or switch to map view to see everything at a glance.

Plan every trip

Group places into collections — "Paris 2026", "Restaurants to try", "Weekend hikes". Mark spots as Want to Go or Visited, then share the whole list with your travel companions.

Discover what's nearby

Sort your saved places by distance from where you are right now. Tap once to open turn-by-turn directions in Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze — driving or walking.

Private by design

Your places are stored in your personal iCloud account — or only on your device if you prefer. We never collect, store, or see your data. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Read our privacy policy →
